# Brand Rules

## Content Type
Animated micro-stories

## Target Audience
Persian-speaking Gen Z and young adults

## Tone
Fast, emotional, mysterious, cinematic

## Visual Rules
- Neon blue
- Purple
- Black
- High contrast
- Glitch effects

## Writing Rules
- One idea per video.
- One emotional payoff.
- No long intros.
- First 2 seconds must hook.
- End must loop.

## CTA Rules
- Ask one comment-trigger question.
- No aggressive selling.
