# Final Publish Check

## Content
- Hook is strong in the first 2 seconds.
- Body is short and clear.
- Payoff is visible.
- Loop ending exists.
- CTA is short and comment-friendly.

## Metadata
- Title is short and curiosity-driven.
- Caption is 1-2 lines.
- Hashtags are relevant and limited.
- Thumbnail text is 3-5 words max.

## Publish Rule
- If all items pass, upload now.
- If 1 or more fail, revise first.
